# Task: stage-e-06-wave-two-planning

## Goal

Define the second bounded Stage E execution wave after the first Support / Job workbench foundation wave completed.

## Project Stage

- Stage: Stage E
- Track: planning across Research, Platform Reliability, and Delivery and Operations

## Why

The first Stage E wave proved that Support and Job can persist durable workbench objects, but those objects are still
closer to readable state than to direct work-driving surfaces. The next bounded wave should make those workbench objects
actionable without expanding Stage E into a broad ticketing or ATS product.

## Scope

- define the second executable Stage E wave
- keep Support case and Job hiring-packet scope bounded around direct workbench actions
- preserve the live public demo as an honest, usable walkthrough surface while those actions are added

## Deliverables

- updates to `docs/prd/STAGE_E_PLAN.md`
- `tasks/stage-e-07-support-case-action-loop.md`
- `tasks/stage-e-08-job-hiring-packet-action-loop.md`
- `tasks/stage-e-09-public-demo-workbench-entry-and-walkthrough.md`
- control-plane updates activating the new Stage E wave

## Acceptance Criteria

- Stage E has one explicit second wave instead of an implicit continuation
- the new wave stays focused on workbench actions rather than broad product-suite ambition
- the control plane points to one concrete next task
